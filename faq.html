Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about FundMentor, pricing, coverage, privacy, and how to get in touch.

What is FundMentor?

FundMentor is an advisor-focused tool that helps you build, analyze, and explain ETF portfolios in minutes. It’s designed to create clarity for advisors and investors and is for educational and informational purposes only.

Is FundMentor free to use?

We provide an online free-to-use version. Please contact us to discuss premium functionality we can add for you and your team.

Does FundMentor provide investment advice?

No. FundMentor does not provide personalized investment advice. Advisors remain fully responsible for their own recommendations and compliance.

Who can use FundMentor?

Financial advisors, RIAs, asset managers, and DIY investors who want clearer portfolios and explanations can all benefit from FundMentor.

What markets and ETFs are covered?

Currently, FundMentor supports U.S. and Canadian ETFs. Coverage will expand over time.

Will FundMentor include stocks as well as ETFs?

Yes. We plan to add stock coverage soon.

Can I import my own ETF list or portfolio?

Yes. FundMentor supports Custom ETF Lists and the Portfolio Analyzer, so you can upload holdings or tailor recommendations to your preferred universe.

Do you store my data when I use the Portfolio Analyzer or Rebalancing Checker?

No. FundMentor does not store any portfolio data. All analysis runs in your session only and disappears when you leave the app.

Do I need to create an account?

No account or login is required for the beta. Everything runs session-based for simplicity.

How do I request a demo or partnership?

Please use the demo request form, and our team will reach out.
