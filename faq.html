Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about what FundMentor does, who it is for, how the demo works, and how to get in touch.

What is FundMentor?

FundMentor is a portfolio planning and review application that helps users build, review, and explain portfolios with more structure and clarity.

Who is it for?

Advisors, wealth teams, model portfolio groups, asset managers, and serious DIY investors who want more than a basic screener.

What can I do in the demo today?

The current demo supports ETF-focused workflows for planning, idea generation, portfolio diagnostics, and rebalancing.

Is FundMentor just an ETF screener?

No. Screening is one part of the workflow, but the product is also designed to support portfolio review, diagnostics, and clearer client conversations.

Is FundMentor free to use?

There is a free online demo version. For deeper use cases, additional functionality, or team access, reach out to discuss a demo or quote.

Does FundMentor provide investment advice?

No. FundMentor is for information and education only. Users remain responsible for their own recommendations, compliance, and suitability decisions.

What markets are covered?

The current version supports U.S. and Canadian ETFs. Broader product coverage is planned over time.

Will FundMentor include stocks and mutual funds?

That is part of the broader direction. The goal is to apply data-driven logic more broadly across additional investment products over time.

Can I upload my own ETF list or portfolio?

Yes. FundMentor supports saved ETF lists and portfolio uploads so you can work with your own universe and review current holdings.

Do you store my portfolio data?

No. Portfolio data used in the Portfolio and Rebalance workflows is processed in-session only and is not stored by FundMentor.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for the demo.

How do I request a demo, quote, or partnership discussion?

Please use the demo request form and include a short note about your use case.
