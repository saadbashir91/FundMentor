Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-25

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use FundMentor:

  • Contact forms: name, email, company, and message when you request a demo or reach out.
  • Usage analytics: we may use privacy-friendly metrics or Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Portfolio inputs: any data entered into the Portfolio Analyzer or Rebalancing Checker is processed in-session only and is not stored by FundMentor.

How we use your information

  • Respond to demo requests and inquiries.
  • Improve site features and user experience.
  • Operate and secure the service.

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for advertising.

Data storage & security

Contact information is retained only as long as necessary to respond to you. Portfolio data entered into our tools is not saved. FundMentor is hosted using GitHub Pages and Streamlit, which may collect their own technical data; please review their policies for details.

Cookies & analytics

If enabled, Google Analytics may place cookies in your browser to measure site usage. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your contact information at any time by using the demo request form and mentioning “Privacy.”

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
