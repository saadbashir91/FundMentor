§ 01
The workflow

Four stages. One decision trail.

Most tools give you a screener. FundMentor gives you the full arc — from shaping the allocation to shipping the paperwork — with every decision documented as you go.

01 · Build

Build

Set the client context, shape the allocation, and create a shortlist. Guardrails and goal-aware logic run in the background.

Model portfolios
02 · Research

Research

Browse the ETF universe, assemble a sandbox, then stress-test it with Monte Carlo and six historical scenarios before it enters a client plan.

Sandbox & simulation
03 · Review

Review

Upload a client's existing portfolio. See strengths, risks, overlap, and the delta to the proposed model. Export a branded proposal PDF.

Current vs proposed
04 · Adjust

Adjust

Compare current holdings against target weights. Surface the trades needed to realign. Tax-aware for taxable accounts.

Drift & rebalancing
§ 02
What you get

Not a screener. A deliverable.

Every session produces something the advisor can actually send or file. These are the four artifacts that come out the other end.

Simulation
01 / 04

Monte Carlo projection

5,000-trial simulation of the portfolio over the planning horizon. Shows probability of success, median outcome, bad-case and good-case tails.

  • Percentile fan chart (5th, 25th, 50th, 75th, 95th)
  • Configurable contributions, withdrawals, and goal
  • Plain-English verdict: strong, reasonable, workable, or shortfall risk
Stress tests
02 / 04

Six historical shocks

How would this portfolio have held up in actual market events? One click runs every scenario and presents them as color-coded cards.

  • 2008 Global Financial Crisis · COVID-19 crash
  • 2022 joint drawdown · +100bps rate shock
  • Dot-com bust · 1970s-style inflation surge
Client PDF
03 / 04

Branded proposal

The one-pager advisors actually email. Current vs proposed side-by-side, delta in fees, yield, positions, and dollar impact. Ready to send in under a minute.

  • Executive summary table
  • Allocation comparison and proposed holdings
  • "Why this proposal" rationale in plain language
Compliance
04 / 04

Investment Policy Statement

A compliance-ready IPS generated in seconds. Target allocation with min/max bands, permitted and prohibited investments, risk guardrails, and a suitability framework keyed to the client's jurisdiction.

  • CIRO CFR for Canada · Reg BI for the United States
  • Rebalancing policy and monitoring schedule
  • Client and advisor signature blocks

§ 04
Regulatory framing

Built around the rules advisors already live by.

Know-Your-Client, Know-Your-Product, Suitability Determination, documented rationale, reasonable alternatives — these aren't afterthoughts in FundMentor. They're how it works.

Canada

CIRO Client Focused Reforms

Recommendations are generated with an auditable suitability rationale. The IPS output is aligned to CIRO CFR's KYC, KYP, and Suitability Determination obligations.

United States

Regulation Best Interest

Documented rationale, reasonable alternatives, and disclosure of material conflicts — generated at the same time as the proposal, not as a separate compliance step afterward.

All jurisdictions

Documentation as output

Every session produces a suitability rationale, a target allocation with bands, and a rebalancing policy. The paperwork isn't homework — it's the default.
