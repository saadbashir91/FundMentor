About FundMentor

Our Mission

To give financial advisors and investors clarity. FundMentor turns complex ETF data into simple, client-ready portfolios — transparent, educational, and easy to explain.

For information and education only — not investment advice.

What we offer

  • Portfolio Builder with transparent guardrails
  • ETF Screener with live, sortable metrics
  • Portfolio Analyzer and Rebalancing Checker
  • Advisor Notes — plain-language explanations tied to your chosen ETFs

How it works

  • Build — Goal-based allocations with ETF recommendations.
  • Analyze — Portfolio metrics, fees, income, and drift checks.
  • Explain — Plain-language notes for client conversations.

Who we serve

  • Independent advisors and RIAs
  • Asset managers and model portfolio teams
  • Educated DIY investors

Data & Privacy

FundMentor does not store any portfolio data. All analysis in the Portfolio Analyzer and Rebalancing Checker runs only in your session and disappears when you leave the app.
