About FundMentor

FundMentor helps advisors and investors design ETF portfolios with clarity. We focus on two things: sound portfolio construction and client‑ready communication.

What we offer

  • Portfolio Builder with transparent guardrails
  • ETF Screener with live, sortable metrics
  • Portfolio Analyzer and Rebalancing Checker
  • Advisor Notes — plain‑language explanations tied to your chosen ETFs

Who we serve

  • Independent advisors and RIAs
  • Asset managers and model portfolio teams
  • Educated DIY investors
